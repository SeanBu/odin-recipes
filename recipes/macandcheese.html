Mac and Cheese

This is the worlds best home made mac and cheese

Who doesn't love mac and cheese? Better yet who doesn't love HOME MADE mac and cheese? After following this recipie you will never go back to buying mac and cheese from a box.

Home made mac and cheese is very simple to make. You can make it ahead of time, freeze it or refigerate to and then reheat it once you are ready to consume. Mac and cheese is a great meal to get your kids involved with making dinner for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spaghetti Elbows

Steps

  1. Boil salted water
  2. Add spaghetti elbows to boiling water
  3. Boil elbows untill almost done
  4. Strain all water from elblows
  5. Add elbows to oven safe dish
  6. Add cheese over elbows in dish
  7. Put in pre heated oven of 350 degrees and bake for 30 minutes or until top is golden brown
