Pizza

This is the worlds best home made mac and cheese

Pizza is arguably one of the most popular food around. What if you could make your own at home and save some money?

This easy to follow recipe will take you through the process of making pizza at your home. You will be using a pre made pizza dough that you can find at any super market to save some time and make things so much easier.

Ingredients

  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Stretch out pizza dough on a floured surfance to an aproximate 12in circle
  2. With a spoon spread around pizza sauce
  3. Add cheese on top of pizza sauce
  4. Add pepperoni to top of pizza
  5. Put pizza in preheated 500 degree oven
  6. Bake until done
